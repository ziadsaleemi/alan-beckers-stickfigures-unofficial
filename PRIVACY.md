# Privacy Policy

Alan Beckers Stickfigures is a local desktop companion app. The app does not
collect, sell, share, or transmit personal data.

The macOS app uses local system information such as display bounds, visible Dock
geometry, and open window frames so stick figures can move around the desktop and
interact with windows. This information stays on the device and is not sent to
the developer or any third party.

Settings and diagnostic logs are stored locally on the user's device. Users can
remove the app's local data by deleting its Application Support folder.

For support, open an issue in this repository:
https://github.com/ziadsaleemi/alan-beckers-stickfigures-unofficial/issues
